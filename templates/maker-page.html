Typhoon

Glenn de Randamie (1984), known as Typhoon, is a Dutch rapper, singer and inspirer born and raised in 't Harde. Inspired by his brother Blaxtar, he started writing at age 12 and joined rap group Rudeteenz at 15, alongside Blaxtar and the well-known Dutch hip-hop formation Opgezwolle.

After winning the Grote Prijs — the biggest hip-hop prize in the Netherlands — Typhoon signed to Top Notch records. In 2007 he released his solo debut Tussen Licht en Lucht, followed by the acclaimed Lobi Da Basi (Surinamese for "Love Rules") in 2014, which made him a festival favourite with both hip-hop and pop audiences. His most recent album, Lichthuis, was released in 2020.

His work radiates hope, connection and the belief that we are enough — we are love.
