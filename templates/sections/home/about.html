The Spirit

A festival, platform, and network that brings people together around art, meaning, and social themes
We develop programs that inspire calm, connection, and wonder in places originally built for that purpose: churches, mosques, synagogues, temples, and special heritage sites
“We bring together as many sources of inspiration as possible and encourage people to dare to dream of a more loving, more connected world. Change begins with touching, experiences that open the heart and create movement from there.”
